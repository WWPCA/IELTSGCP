IELTS GenAI Prep - Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At IELTS GenAI Prep, we are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring your experience with our services is as safe and secure as possible. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, region, test preference, and target score
  • Assessment Data: Written responses, speaking recordings, assessment results, and feedback
  • Payment Information: We do not store credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is conducted securely via Stripe.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, including features used, time spent, and assessment attempts
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services, including GenAI assessment using TrueScore® and Elaris® technologies
  • To process payments and manage your account
  • To communicate with you about your account, products, and updates
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize our platform
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

One-Time Assessment Data Usage

For our one-time assessment products (including speaking-only packages):

  • Your assessment data (written responses or speaking recordings) is processed by our GenAI assessment systems to generate feedback and scores
  • We retain your assessment responses and results in your account for your reference
  • We may use anonymized assessment data to improve our assessment algorithms and systems
  • We do not share your individual assessment responses with third parties except as required by law

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Companies that help us deliver our services (payment processors, cloud services, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Account information is retained as long as you maintain an active account
  • Assessment results and feedback are retained indefinitely to allow for long-term progress tracking
  • Payment records are retained for the period required by applicable financial regulations

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@ieltsgenaiprep.com.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 16, please contact us immediately.

International Data Transfers

We operate globally and may transfer your information to countries with different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information when transferred internationally.

Security Measures

We implement technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data storage practices

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through our platform.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@ieltsgenaiprep.com.
