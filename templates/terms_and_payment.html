By purchasing IELTS assessment products, you agree to the following terms:

  2. Assessment fees are charged as a one-time payment upon confirmation of purchase.
  3. There are no subscriptions - all purchases are individual, one-time payments.
  4. Assessments never expire - you may use them at any time after purchase.
  5. Once an assessment is completed, you retain permanent access to your results and feedback.

  7. If your assessment is interrupted due to internet connectivity issues, you may restart it once without using an additional assessment credit.
