Test Format

IELTS Online is the Academic version of IELTS that you can take from the comfort of your home or another suitable location. It offers the same trusted IELTS quality with the convenience of online delivery.

The test consists of four sections: Listening (30 minutes + 10 minutes transfer time), Reading (60 minutes), Writing (60 minutes), and Speaking (11-14 minutes via video call). The total test time is approximately 2 hours and 45 minutes.

Test Sections

  • Listening: 30 minutes + 10 minutes transfer time
  • Reading: 60 minutes
  • Writing: 60 minutes
  • Speaking: 11-14 minutes via video call with a certified IELTS Examiner

Technical Requirements

  • Computer: Windows 10+ or Mac OS 10.11+
  • Browser: Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge
  • Internet: Stable connection with minimum 1.5 Mbps speed
  • Webcam: Working camera with clear image quality
  • Microphone: Working and clear audio input
  • Speakers: Working audio output (headphones allowed)
  • Smartphone: Required for room scanning during check-in

Test Security

IELTS Online uses advanced security measures to ensure the integrity of the test. This includes AI proctoring technology combined with human monitoring, identity verification using government-issued ID, and room scanning to ensure no unauthorized materials are present.

Important Security Notes

  • You must be alone in the room for the entire test.
  • No unauthorized materials are allowed in the test room.
  • You cannot leave the camera view during the test.
  • Your test will be recorded for security purposes.
  • You may be asked to show your room using your smartphone camera before the test begins.

Advantages of IELTS Online

Test from Home

Take the test from the comfort of your own home or any private location with a stable internet connection. No need to travel to a test center.

Flexible Scheduling

More test dates and time slots are available compared to in-person testing, giving you greater flexibility in scheduling your test.

Same IELTS Quality

The test has the same content, format, and level of difficulty as the in-person IELTS Academic test, and is equally recognized by institutions worldwide.

Fast Results

Receive your results within 3-5 days, which is typically faster than paper-based testing.

Preparation Tips for IELTS Online

Before Test Day

  • Take a practice test online to familiarize yourself with the format.
  • Check that your computer meets all technical requirements.
  • Test your microphone, camera, and internet connection.
  • Prepare a suitable room that is quiet, well-lit, and private.
  • Have your ID document ready (passport is recommended).

On Test Day

  • Log in 30 minutes before your scheduled test time.
  • Clear your desk of all items except what's allowed.
  • Have a clear bottle of water if needed.
  • Ensure your face is well-lit and clearly visible.
  • Follow all instructions from the online proctor.

How to Book IELTS Online

  1. Visit the official IELTS website or contact your local IELTS test center.
  2. Select "IELTS Online" as your test type.
  3. Choose your preferred date and time.
  4. Complete the registration process and make payment.
  5. Receive confirmation email with instructions for the test day.
  6. Perform the system check before your test date to ensure your computer meets all requirements.

Availability Note

IELTS Online is available in most countries, but not all. Check the official IELTS website for availability in your region. Also note that not all organizations accept IELTS Online scores, so verify with your receiving organization before booking.
