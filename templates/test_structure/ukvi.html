IELTS for UKVI (UK Visas and Immigration)

{{ test_info.description }}

Test Format

IELTS for UKVI has the same content, format, and level of difficulty as the standard IELTS test. The difference is that it's specifically approved for UK visa and immigration purposes and is conducted in UKVI-approved centers with increased security measures.

Key Information

  • Two versions available: You can take either IELTS Academic for UKVI or IELTS General Training for UKVI, depending on your visa requirements.
  • Special security: The test is conducted with enhanced security measures, including biometric testing (a photograph and finger scan).
  • Approved centers: The test must be taken at a location specially approved by UK Visas and Immigration.
  • Recognized globally: IELTS for UKVI is recognized not just by UK Visas and Immigration but also by universities, employers, and immigration authorities around the world.

Which Test Should I Take?

The test you need depends on your visa type:

  • Tier 1, 2, or 4 visas: Either Academic or General Training, depending on your specific circumstances
  • For study at degree level and above: Academic IELTS for UKVI
  • For work or study below degree level: General Training IELTS for UKVI
  • For family, settlement or ILR: Usually IELTS Life Skills (A1, A2 or B1 level)

How to Book IELTS for UKVI

  1. Check which version of the test you need for your visa application.
  2. Find an approved UKVI test center near you through the official IELTS website.
  3. Book your test online or through your local test center, specifying that you need IELTS for UKVI.
  4. Be prepared to provide biometric data (photograph and finger scan) on the test day.
  5. Bring your passport as identification on the test day.
