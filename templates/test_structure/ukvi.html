IELTS for UKVI (UK Visas and Immigration)

Purpose

IELTS for UKVI is a Secure English Language Test (SELT) approved by UK Visas and Immigration for visa applications to the United Kingdom.

What is IELTS for UKVI?

IELTS for UKVI (UK Visas and Immigration) is a version of the IELTS test that is specially designed to meet the requirements of UK immigration applications. It is a Secure English Language Test (SELT) approved by UK Visas and Immigration for visa applications to the UK.

Important Note: The content, format, level of difficulty, scoring system, and assessment criteria for IELTS for UKVI are exactly the same as the regular IELTS test. The only difference is that the test is administered with increased security measures and is taken at approved UKVI test centers.

IELTS for UKVI Test Formats

IELTS for UKVI is available in three formats:

IELTS Academic for UKVI

This is identical to the standard IELTS Academic test but administered in UKVI-approved test centers. It's suitable for those applying to study at undergraduate or postgraduate levels or seeking professional registration in the UK.

Sections:

  • Listening (30 minutes + 10 minutes transfer time)
  • Reading (60 minutes)
  • Writing (60 minutes)
  • Speaking (11-14 minutes)
View Academic Test Details

IELTS General Training for UKVI

This is identical to the standard IELTS General Training test but administered in UKVI-approved test centers. It's suitable for those applying to train or study below degree level, work, or migrate to the UK.

Sections:

  • Listening (30 minutes + 10 minutes transfer time)
  • Reading (60 minutes)
  • Writing (60 minutes)
  • Speaking (11-14 minutes)
View General Training Test Details

IELTS Life Skills

IELTS Life Skills is a specific test format designed for those applying for UK visas in the 'family of a settled person' or 'settlement' categories.

Sections: Speaking and Listening only

Available at levels:

  • A1 (for specific visas like 'family of a settled person')
  • B1 (for settlement or naturalization)

Duration: 16-22 minutes

UKVI Specific Requirements

When taking an IELTS for UKVI test, there are specific requirements that differ from the standard IELTS test:

  • Test Centers: You must take the test at a center that is specifically approved to administer IELTS for UKVI.
  • Biometric Data: You will need to have your photograph taken on the day of the test. This photograph will appear on your Test Report Form (TRF).
  • Identity Verification: Enhanced identity verification procedures are in place.
  • Test Report Form: The Test Report Form has 'SELT' written on it to indicate it was taken as a Secure English Language Test.
  • Test Booking: When booking your test, make sure you specifically select 'IELTS for UKVI'.
Important: A standard IELTS test cannot be used for UK visa applications that require a SELT. You must take the IELTS for UKVI test.

Which UKVI Visa Applications Require IELTS?

Different UK visa types require different English language tests and minimum scores:

Visa Type Required Test Minimum Level
Tier 1 Visa (Entrepreneur) IELTS Academic or General Training for UKVI B1 (at least 4.0 in all skills)
Tier 2 Visa (Skilled Worker) IELTS Academic or General Training for UKVI B1 (at least 4.0 in all skills)
Tier 4 Visa (Student) - Degree level and above IELTS Academic for UKVI B2 (at least 5.5 in all skills)
Tier 4 Visa (Student) - Below degree level IELTS Academic for UKVI B1 (at least 4.0 in all skills)
Family Visa (2.5 year route) IELTS Life Skills A1 (speaking and listening only)
Settlement (5 year route) IELTS Life Skills B1 (speaking and listening only)
British Citizenship/Naturalization IELTS Life Skills B1 (speaking and listening only)
Note: Requirements may change. Always check the official UK government website for the most up-to-date information on visa English language requirements.

CEFR Levels and IELTS Scores

UK visa requirements often refer to Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) levels. Here's how IELTS scores correspond to CEFR levels:
