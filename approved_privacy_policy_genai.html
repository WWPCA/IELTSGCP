When you create an account, we collect:

b. Assessment Content

When you use our Platform, we collect:

When you contact us for support, we collect the information you provide in your messages.

Important: We do NOT directly collect or store your payment information. All payments for our products and services (e.g., practice modules, mock tests) are processed securely through the respective Apple App Store (for iOS users) or Google Play Store (for Android users).

When you make a purchase through our mobile application, your payment details are handled by Apple or Google in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms of service. We only receive confirmation from the app stores that a purchase has been made and which specific product was purchased, but we do not receive your credit card number or other sensitive financial information.

Please refer to the privacy policies of Apple and Google for details on how they handle your payment information.

6. Automated Decision-Making and AI Processing

Important Notice: Our Platform uses artificial intelligence (AI) to assess your IELTS practice responses and provide feedback. This involves automated decision-making that may significantly affect your practice scores and recommendations.

How AI Processing Works:

  • Assessment Algorithms: We use Google Gemini AI models to evaluate your speaking and writing responses based on IELTS assessment criteria.
  • Scoring Logic: AI algorithms analyze your responses for fluency, coherence, lexical resource, grammatical range, accuracy, and pronunciation (for speaking).
  • Feedback Generation: The AI generates personalized feedback, identifies areas for improvement, and provides practice recommendations.

Your Rights Regarding AI Processing:

You have the right to:

  • Receive information about the logic involved in automated decision-making
  • Understand how AI assessments are generated
  • Be informed that AI scores are practice indicators only and not official IELTS scores
  • Contact us at helpdesk@ieltsaiprep.com with questions about AI processing

8. Your Data Protection Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR for EEA residents), you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data and receive a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: You can request that we limit how we use your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your personal data based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You can file a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at helpdesk@ieltsaiprep.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by law.
