                        IELTS Speaking Part 1 Questions and Prompts
                        
                        Official IELTS Speaking Part 1 Assessment Context:
                        Introduction and Interview (4-5 minutes): General questions about familiar topics like home, family, work, studies, and interests.
                        
                        Authentic Speaking Questions for Part 1:
                        
1. Tell me about your favorite hobby and why you enjoy it.
2. Tell me about your job. What responsibilities do you have?
3. What do you like or dislike about your studies?
4. Would you prefer to work in a large company or a small company? Why?
5. Can you describe the place where you live?
6. What kind of accommodation do you live in?
7. What changes would you like to make to your home?
8. Describe your hometown. What is it known for?
9. Is your hometown a good place for tourists to visit? Why or why not?
10. How has your hometown changed in recent years?
11. What activities do you enjoy doing in your free time?
12. Do you prefer indoor or outdoor activities? Why?
13. How important is it to have hobbies?
14. How often do you use computers or technology in your daily life?
15. What impact does technology have on your work or studies?
16. Do you think people rely too much on technology nowadays?
17. What kind of places do you like to visit on vacation?
18. Do you prefer traveling alone or with other people? Why?
19. What's the most interesting journey you've ever taken?
                        
                        Assessment Guidelines for Part 1:
                        - Focus on natural conversation flow
                        - Evaluate fluency, coherence, lexical resource, grammar, pronunciation
                        - Questions should elicit appropriate language level for IELTS assessment
                        - Responses should be evaluated against official IELTS band descriptors
