Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your information

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to IELTS GenAI Prep, featuring TrueScore® and ClearScore® - the world's ONLY GenAI assessor tools for IELTS test preparation. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our IELTS preparation services, including through our website and mobile applications.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you disagree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our Service:

  • Personal Data: Information that can identify you, such as your name, email address, and account credentials
  • Assessment Data: Your test responses, scores, feedback, and performance analytics
  • Audio Recordings: When you take speaking assessments, we process audio recordings to provide AI-powered feedback using Amazon Nova Sonic technology
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, including study patterns and progress tracking
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and app usage analytics
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed securely through Apple App Store and Google Play Store

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining our assessment services
  • Processing your speaking and writing assessments using advanced AI technology
  • Generating personalized feedback and performance analytics
  • Managing your account and assessment purchases
  • Improving our AI assessment algorithms and service quality
  • Communicating with you about your account and service updates
  • Ensuring the security and integrity of our platform
  • Complying with legal obligations and tax requirements

4. Audio Data Processing

For speaking assessments using our ClearScore® technology:

  • Audio recordings are processed in real-time using Amazon Nova Sonic AI
  • Recordings are temporarily stored during assessment processing
  • Audio data is automatically deleted after assessment completion
  • Only transcripts and assessment results are permanently retained
  • We do not store or retain voice data for any purpose beyond assessment processing

5. Data Retention

We retain different types of data for different periods:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for legal compliance periods after deletion
  • Assessment Results: Stored permanently to provide you with ongoing access to your progress and feedback
  • Audio Recordings: Processed temporarily and automatically deleted after assessment completion
  • Payment Information: Retained according to financial regulations and app store requirements
  • Inactive Accounts: Automatically deleted after 24 months of inactivity

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only when necessary:

  • AI Service Providers: Amazon Web Services for Nova Sonic and Nova Micro AI processing
  • App Store Partners: Apple App Store and Google Play Store for payment processing
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS services for secure data storage and processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and user safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

7. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure AWS infrastructure with multiple availability zones
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication for all data access
  • Automatic data backup and disaster recovery procedures

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request export of your assessment data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us through our mobile app support system.

9. International Data Transfers

Our services operate globally using AWS infrastructure. Your data may be processed in:

  • United States (primary AWS region)
  • Europe (EU-West region)
  • Asia-Pacific (AP-Southeast region)

All international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and AWS security standards.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
